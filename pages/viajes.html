26 days in Porto

Mayo 28th 2023 Escrito

First Week;


More than two weeks have passed since I completed the Piscine project in Porto, spanning a period of 26 days from October 12, 2023, to January 5, 2024. This period was marked by intense and demanding days, each surpassing the previous one in terms of activity.

Given that the Piscine project took place in a city different from my current residence in Leria, I had to arrange accommodation before commencing work. However, this task proved to be somewhat challenging, leading me to opt for an Airbnb, which unfortunately did not meet my expectations.

The onset of the first week was characterized by a rapid pace. Initial days were dedicated to acquainting myself with people, understanding the school's concept, familiarizing myself with the operational procedures, and acquiring the necessary knowledge to progress in the projects. Consequently, my daily commitment extended beyond 12 hours at the school. This initial week was a period of personal disarray, where I struggled with time management, dietary routines, and the continuity of my fitness regimen. This imbalance took a toll on my emotional well-being. Despite these personal challenges, my academic endeavors were fruitful, and I successfully navigated through the week, culminating in a satisfactory performance on the test.
